Redefining consumer retail in Africa

The word kudi is from the hausa language spoken across sub-saharan Africa, which means ‘money’. Thus the name “KudiGo” translates to ‘money-on-the-go’.

KudiGo Storefront is an all-in-one technology solution for informal and micro retail businesses, enabling them to effectively and efficiently manage and run their business by aggregating and integrating the retail value chain. It is a mobile-first, complete retail business suite which integrates a retail engine, accounting engine, and payment processing. This data will transform retailers' ability to access credit and grow their businesses more profitably.

KudiGo RapidGate is designed for distributors and suppliers who provide goods directly to retailers in Africa. It offers access to previously unknown stock management and transactional data, enabling increased visibility across retail and distribution networks.

KudiGo is looking to partner with financial services companies and FMCG manufacturers across Africa.
